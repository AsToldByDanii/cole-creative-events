Our Story

More Than Balloons.
It's Your Moment.

Erica Cole turned a passion for celebration into a full-service creative studio. For years, she has transformed ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences — from intimate baby showers to grand birthday galas — pouring artistry, heart, and meticulous attention to detail into every single event.

For Erica, event design isn't a job — it's her calling. She believes every milestone deserves a backdrop as beautiful as the moment itself, and she won't rest until your vision becomes a reality that takes your breath away.
